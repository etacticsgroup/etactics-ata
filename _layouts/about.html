Feature-rich services

Here at Automata, our services are purpose-built for Web 3.0. Witness, our private governance solution, was launched and introduced to the industry in March 2021, and has since gone on to be supported by various blockchains including Plasm, Clover Finance and Crust Network, with further integration on Ethereum and Binance Smart Chain.

We’re also working hard on building an invisibility cloak for the Dark Forest characterized by the highly transparent, highly adversarial environment of public blockchains. Check out Conveyor, our MEV Minimization solution.

Non-intrusive

Among other things, we’re committed to making the deployment of our solutions a seamless one. This means near-zero modification for any and every dApps interface and logic. No distractions from the Web3.0 vision, no holding back the product getting into the hands of users.

We’ve spent a lot of time and effort building our infrastructure so you won’t have to repeat the process. Why not give it a try?

Universal compatibility

We strive to create a more collaborative and connected Web3.0 ecosystem and part of this relates to the design of our services that are compatible with existing dApps on multiple chains. As we work to accelerate the development of Web3.0 in impactful, practical ways, integration and scalability must happen at a global level.

If you’re keen to contribute to this future, more hands on deck is always welcome - our Github is open-source!
